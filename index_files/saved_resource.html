FAQ
What is a CTF event?

Capture The Flag (CTF) is a hacking competition where you solve challenges to find hidden flags. It's like a treasure hunt, but with more code and less pirates!

How do I join the fun?

Simply register on our site, and prepare to unleash your inner hacker! No experience? No problem! We’ll teach you the ropes, or at least how to tie a few knots.

Is it safe to participate?

Absolutely! We promote ethical hacking. Just don’t try this at home unless you want to explain to your mom why her Wi-Fi is down.

What prizes can I win?

Bragging rights, epic swag, and maybe a trophy that’ll make your friends jealous!

Can I compete solo or in teams?

Both! Go solo if you’re a lone wolf, or team up for double the fun and chaos!
