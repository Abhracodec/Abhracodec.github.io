Historical Insights

Throughout history, various methods have been used for secure communication and information protection. One notable method from ancient times is the use of covert messages written on the heads of slaves or hidden within common items. This practice highlights the lengths to which people have gone to protect their secrets and the evolution of cryptographic techniques.
